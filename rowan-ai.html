Rowan

2025

Built for accounting and finance teams, Rowan is an AI agent that automates reconciliations, streamlines financial reporting and accelerates client delivery — all within Excel.

Rowan is an AI assistant built directly into Excel, designed for accountants and finance teams. It can import and interpret data, explain formulas, automate workflows, and streamline audit and reporting tasks.

I’ve been involved from inception, shaping Rowan’s product direction, brand identity, and go-to-market strategy. A key focus was differentiating it from generic AI tools by building an accountant-first positioning and a strong brand.

The project pushed me beyond my usual scope of work: my activities frequently involved motion design, video editing, digital marketing (both paid and organic), front-end development, AI eval and prototyping.
