Rowan

2025

Built for accounting and finance teams, Rowan is an AI agent that automates reconciliations, streamlines financial reporting and accelerates client delivery — all within Excel.

Rowan is an AI agent built directly into Excel, designed for accountants and finance teams. It helps users import and clean data, audit spreadsheets, automate recurring tasks, and get answers in context — all without leaving the workflow they already rely on. The product combines conversational AI with structured, reliable tools that feel native to Excel.

As the founding designer, I shaped Rowan’s product direction, interface, and core workflows from day one. My focus was on making AI practical for non-technical professionals by pairing flexible chat with deterministic features for tasks like import, formatting, and auditing. I worked closely with the CEO and engineering team to define how the agent should behave, feel, and integrate into real accounting processes.

I also created Rowan’s brand identity — name, personality, and visual system — to differentiate it from generic AI tools and support its accountant-first positioning. Alongside design, I contributed to marketing, onboarding, and growth, ensuring consistency across the entire user journey and helping the product reach market quickly.
