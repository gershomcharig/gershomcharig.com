Pactio

2022 — 2025

By digitising private capital transactions from deal creation to exit, Pactio streamlines workflows for private equity professionals, tax advisers, lawyers, and fund administrators.

Pactio is a platform that digitises private-capital workflows, helping private equity, tax, legal, and fund-admin teams manage complex transactions from deal creation to exit. It streamlines processes that traditionally rely on fragmented spreadsheets, documents, and manual review, offering a structured, AI-enhanced environment tailored to the needs of professional services.

As the founding designer, I joined at seed stage to create the MVP and set the product’s design direction. I designed the core user experience that helped land Pactio’s first enterprise clients and contributed to securing a $14M Series A. I also built a scalable design system that supports highly complex workflows, enabling the platform to handle transactions totalling more than $10.7B in enterprise value.

My role extended across product, design, and collaboration with founders, engineers, Big 4 advisers, law firms, and top-tier PE teams. This ensured the platform met the industry's highest standards of usability, security, and compliance while supporting rapid iteration in a fast-moving domain.
