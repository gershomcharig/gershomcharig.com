001 About

I’m a product designer based in London. I’ve worked for startups for over 14 years, often as a funding member, helping them launch products and scale. I use my expertise and vision to guide founders through the process of visualising their ideas, discovering what to build, creating their first MVP, and finding product-market fit. Occasionally, I write about the founding designer role and technology.

I’m currently design lead of the founding team at Pactio, building cutting-edge technology for private equity firms. Previously, I worked at Palantir, where I helped the business development team launch 29 new commercial projects. Before that, I’ve been the founding designer of several fintech startups.

I also advise founders who want to incorporate human-centred design into their business.

Reach out to me at hello@gershomcharig.com or @GershomCharig.
