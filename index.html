/about_me

Gershom Charig, founding designer. I’ve been helping startups launch and scale products for over 14 years, often as a founding member. I use my expertise and vision to guide founders through the process of visualising their ideas, discovering what to build, creating their first MVP, and finding product-market fit. Occasionally, I write about the founding designer role and technology.

I’m currently lead product designer and founding member at Pactio, building cutting-edge technology for private equity firms. Previously, I worked at Palantir, where I helped the business development team launch 29 new commercial projects. Before that, I’ve been the founding designer of several fintech startups.

I also advise founders who want to incorporate human-centred design into their business.
